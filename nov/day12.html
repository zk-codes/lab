The Roles They Play

Cinzel commands attention. Its classical letterforms announce sections and titles with the weight of carved stone. It speaks rarely, but decisively.

Lora carries the narrative. Contemporary yet rooted in tradition, it balances readability with character. It's the voice you're hearing now.

JetBrains Mono handles the technical. When we need precision or metadata, the monospace steps in. It's the quiet specialist.

"The details are not the details. They make the design."

— Charles Eames

Three fonts. Three purposes. The constraint isn't a limitation - it's a composition. Each font knows its role, and together they create a page that feels intentional rather than assembled.
