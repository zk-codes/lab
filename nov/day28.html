intentional imbalance

Nothing on this page is centered. No element mirrors another. The layout leans, shifts, refuses to settle into comfortable symmetry.

Why Asymmetry?

Symmetry is safe. It signals order, stability, predictability. We default to it because it feels "correct."

But asymmetry creates energy. It suggests movement, tension, life. The eye travels differently across an unbalanced page.

Tension Creates Interest

Visual weight distributed unevenly pulls the eye across the composition.

Movement

Asymmetry implies action, not rest.

Uniqueness

Balanced layouts blend together. Unbalanced ones stand apart.

The Shapes Scatter

"Perfect symmetry is the symmetry of a grave." — Asymmetry introduces life because life is never perfectly balanced.

The constraint forbids centering, mirroring, and equal distribution. What emerges is a page that breathes differently. It may feel uncomfortable. That discomfort is the point.
