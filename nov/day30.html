Asymmetry
Constraints don't limit creativity. They focus it. The blank page is harder than the ruled one.

This final page uses everything. Gradients, animations, backdrop filters, multiple fonts, absolute positioning, z-index, shadows, transforms. Every tool the previous twenty-nine days prohibited, deployed here in celebration.

But you know something now that you didn't know before: you don't need all of this. You can build beautiful, functional, meaningful pages with far less.

No-Vember is over. The constraints are lifted.
What will you choose to use? What will you choose to leave behind?
