Without Prose

a story told in fragments

The Rules

  • No paragraph tags allowed
  • Lists become our sentences
  • Headings carry the weight of chapters
  • Blockquotes speak when we cannot

What We Discover

  1. Language bends to its container
  2. Rhythm emerges from repetition
  3. The list imposes order on chaos
  4. Constraints do not silence; they focus
  5. Poetry lives in the spaces between
The web was built on documents. Documents were built on structure. Structure does not require the paragraph. The paragraph is a convention. Conventions can be broken.

Definitions

Constraint
A limitation that reveals hidden possibilities
List
A series of items, each complete in itself
Fragment
A piece that suggests a whole
