Effortless for the stones, the birds, and the trees.

Yet for the homo sapiens?

Near impossible. Tell me something I don't know already.

And yet, in this confounding limitation...could we find something else?

Not a mere substitute, or pointless aspiring that'll never lead anywhere...

But an opening. A reckoning.

To know one's limitations, and overcome the ones that matter.

To live with caution, but not be suffocated by fear.

To treat oneself and others with grace, yet accept the falibility of the soul.

This...liquiditiy.

This is the human form of the solid state.
