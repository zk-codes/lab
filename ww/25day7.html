Optimization, Or There Lack Of

So many things are hidden underneath our understanding.

Lurking within the layers of the subconcious. In existence while we're unaware.

Sometimes we know this, and sometimes we don't.

And I'm unsure whether that's supposed to be a good thing.

Like most things in life, it just is...until you think about it.

Yet, you have that beautiful mind of yours for a reason.

An optimal life, taken far too literally, risks being drowned in the world's uncomprimising unpredictability. Force your will upon it too much, and you'll be dashed across the rocks.

And yet...optimization? Taken with a gentle moderation?

Maybe, just maybe...the subterranean will at last make sense.

If only we're prepared, at last, to let the perfect optimal slip from our grasp.
