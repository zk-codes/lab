Don't Forget To Blink

In the electric endless haze, the cursor pulses, the notifications flash, the windows flicker.

Everything blinks except us.

The artificial version is a call to attention, to a haphazard presence, to remain forever wide-eyed. The physical begs us to return. To disconnection, to pausing, to...ourselves.

Don't fight it. Don't forget it.

Let your eyes rest. Let the world go dark for just a moment. In that fraction of a second, you might discover what it feels like to be more than a node in the network.

Because you're alive. Remember that.
