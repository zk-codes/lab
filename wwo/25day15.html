140k Secrets In A Library Card

The library? It isn't just a building full of books. It's the Unicode standard, in physical form. Every single shelf? A code page. Every aisle? A character block.

Like the internet, its complementary successor, it goes on for a glorious forever.

Whether on paper or in binary, the symbols await my brain to render them. Pulling out a book or webpage translates strange pictograms into vivid truth.
