Ekphrastic Cartography

Maps are the edges of reality. Canvases of attempted meaning. Order for a chaotic world.

Sensemaking of the vast spacetime perhaps we'll never truly understand.

Wayfinding as an art form, organizing topography as a philosophical exercise.Every choice we make has ramifications for our wider world. Care is a fleeting currency nowadays.

This good earth and the stardust we come from and become doesn't exist for us. We exist for it.

The end of something only exists if we decide it does.

Made by Zachary Kai, for Weird Web October.

Typset in UnifrakturMaguntia, by j. 'mach' wust. Background is a world map by Samuel Dunn, made in 1794. Border is from a drawing by Francis Seymour Haden, called Bark Refitting.
